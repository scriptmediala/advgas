About Us

The company was founded in 1989 by Frank Beels. After decades in the industry he saw a market need for an Independent welding supply and gas distributor that would bring back the high level of personal service that customers desire, but cannot get from large corporate suppliers or even big independent stores that subscribe to a or corporate mentality. The hobbyists, homeowners, tinkerers and weekend warriors required better service as well as the high-tech electronics, motorsports, labs and custom fabrication commercial accounts. We at Advanced Gas Products pride ourselves on providing a superior level of service to all customers, both large and small. Our customers are part of our family and we treat them as such.

When customers need product there's no, "Sorry, your delivery day was yesterday." Your delivery day is Everyday at Advanced Gas Products because we are the only welding supply in Orange County to offer Same Day Delivery. Top technical help is provided with over 50 years of combined industry experience and our modern facility stocks hundreds of product lines to cater to your every need.
